Site Plan: ElsonsKitchen

A Mesmerizing Taste of Nigerian Dishes

Site Name

Name: ElsonsKitchen

Reason: This name combines a personal touch with a professional culinary identity. It positions the brand as a welcoming kitchen where the rich, authentic flavors of Lagos are celebrated and promoted.

Optional Domain: elsonskitchen-lagos.com

Site Purpose

ElsonsKitchen is designed to help a growing restaurant in Lagos expand its reach and modernize its operations. The site provides an engaging platform for customers to explore traditional Nigerian meals (like Jollof Rice and Egusi Soup) and facilitates easy online ordering to increase sales and business growth.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: "I’m hosting a small gathering and need to order authentic Lagos-style Jollof Rice and Suya for 5 people. How can I place an order online and confirm the price?"
  • Scenario 2: "I’ve heard about the vibrant food culture in Lagos but I'm not familiar with Egusi soup. Where can I find a description of what’s in it before I order?"

Project Pages

  • Home: Warm greeting, intro to Lagos flavors, and must-try dish highlights.
  • Menu: Detailed meal list with descriptions, prices, and interactive "Order Now" popups.
  • About/Place Order: The story of the kitchen and the simulated checkout experience.

Color Scheme

The palette is inspired by the Nigerian flag and the vibrant, warm energy of Lagos markets.

Primary (#024228) - Brand & Trust
  • This color is going to to be used on the “header” and “footer” of the page and for for key brand sections
    • Secondary (#FFB703) - Energy & Warmth
  • This color is going to be used on the Call buttons: “Contact Us,” “Subscribe,” or “Book Now” — high visibility etc.
    • Accent (#7c0c0c) - Appetite & Action
  • A bold, deep red that signals urgency and importance and will be used to style “hover effects,” “Call-to-Action (CTA) Buttons”.

    • Typography

    Headings: 'Playfair Display'

    Selected to give the restaurant a sophisticated and mesmerizing feel, suitable for showcasing the text on high-quality dishes.

    Body Copy: 'Source Sans 3'

    Selected for its modern and clean look, ensuring that menu descriptions and order forms are easy for customers to navigate on any device.
